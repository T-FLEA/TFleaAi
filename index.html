About T-FLEA AI

T-FLEA AI (The Free Learning and Education Agency) is an innovative AI-powered tutor designed to make education accessible to everyone. It helps students overcome the challenges of understanding difficult subjects by providing customized learning experiences. T-FLEA AI offers a range of tools such as interactive lessons, quizzes, 3D visualizations, and easy-to-understand explanations. It also assists teachers in creating more engaging lessons and researchers in organizing their work.
