About T-FLEA AI

T-FLEA AI (The Free Learning and Education Agency) is a revolutionary AI-powered tutor designed to make education accessible to everyone. It helps students who face challenges with difficult subjects but cannot afford a tutor. T-FLEA AI is a personal tutor, offering interactive and in-depth learning experiences, including quizzes, 2D/3D visualizations, and easy-to-understand lessons with book references for further inspiration. It also assists teachers in creating better lesson plans and helps researchers organize their work.
