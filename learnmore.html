Comprehensive File System

The AI features an innovative file system designed for organised and personalised learning. It includes four distinct sub-files:

  • Classroom: A virtual learning space where the AI delivers lessons on specific subjects using interactive and engaging methods.
  • Teacher’s Desk: A dedicated space for students to ask questions, receive in-depth answers, and gain clarity on complex topics.
  • Exam Hall: Offers various assessment types such as quizzes, timed and non-timed tests, flash tests, and practice questions to evaluate and reinforce understanding.
  • Home: A personalised area for creating notes, flashcards, and organising study materials to aid self-directed learning.

  • Incorporates videos and references to enhance conceptual clarity and engagement.

Interactive Learning Environment

  • The AI actively engages learners by asking questions during lessons to reinforce understanding and maintain attention.
  • Encourages students to think critically and test their knowledge in real-time.

Flexible Lesson Timings

  • Adapts the duration and structure of lessons to suit individual student needs, ensuring efficient and comfortable learning sessions.

Text-to-Speech Functionality

  • Supports audio-based lessons to accommodate diverse learning preferences and provide an alternative to traditional reading.

Curated Book References

  • Provides a curated list of recommended books for further reading and deeper exploration of topics, inspiring students to broaden their horizons.

Teacher Support

  • Assists teachers in designing lessons that are easier to understand and more engaging for students, acting as a valuable educational partner.

Research Assistance

Emotional Intelligence

Mascot Character

AI-Generated Personalised Study Plans
